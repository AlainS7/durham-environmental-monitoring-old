What is Air Quality Index (AQI)?

The AQI is a standardized way to communicate air quality levels:

  • 0-50 Good - Perfect for outdoor activities
  • 51-100 Moderate - Acceptable for most people
  • 101-150 Unhealthy for Sensitive Groups
  • 151+ Unhealthy - Limit outdoor exposure

What We Measure

PM2.5: Fine particles that can penetrate deep into lungs

PM10: Larger particles from dust, pollen, and smoke

Weather: Temperature, humidity, wind speed, and pressure

Data is collected from research-grade sensors across Durham
